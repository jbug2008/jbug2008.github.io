Lillian: The New Prophecy of Hogwarts

Lillian: The New Prophecy of Hogwarts by Madame Hermione Weasley and advised by Laura Granger is a quick but changing read. It starts by showing who Lillian Rige was before she went to Hogwarts. Her parents were feared wizards with a deadly secret. Lillian escaped them at Hogwarts. She made friends, found better parents, and saved lives. Read on to find out about Lillian Alice Rige-Weasley-Potter's legacy.
